Star Trek Breakout

The Backstory:

The Backstory: It’s Stardate [-27] 06190.00, and you are on the USS Enterprise as Ensign Bob. Today’s mission is to deliver an antidote for a virus. All the preparations are finished, and you get ready to go into warp speed. But the ship doesn’t move. You run to the engineering deck and see that Klingons are running away with all the dilithium crystals, the power source of the warp core. Luckily, you know that the Klingons like to hide stuff in lockable boxes. You have 40 minutes to recover the stolen crystals before the warp core explodes.
